Shanaz Driving School is a driving school based in Halifax, United Kingdom (2 Aked's Rd, Halifax HX1 2TR), offering driving lessons with a highly rated instructor. The website should present services, reviews, contact details, and directions for potential learners.

### Overall Aesthetic & Vibe

- **Tone:** Friendly, trustworthy, and professional; focused on helping new drivers feel confident and safe.
- **Mood:** Calm and reassuring with a sense of progress and achievement (learning to drive, passing the test).

### Typography
- Use a clean, highly legible sans-serif for body text (e.g. **Nunito**, **Karla**, or **Maven Pro** from Google Fonts) to keep the feel approachable and modern.
- Pair with a slightly more characterful sans-serif for headings (e.g. **Baloo 2** or **Quicksand**) to add warmth and friendliness.
- Headings: bold, rounded and clear; body: regular weight, high contrast for easy reading.

### Color & Theme
- Overall theme: **light and bright**.
- Primary hue: a confident **blue** (clear, non-muddy) to signal trust and reliability.
- Accent hue: a fresh **yellow or lime** accent for call-to-actions and highlights (booking lessons, contact buttons) to suggest energy and progress.
- Neutrals: soft off-white and light warm grey backgrounds for sections, with dark navy/charcoal for text.
- Use CSS variables for colors and spacing to keep the system consistent.

### Imagery & Iconography
- Photography: real or realistic imagery of cars, roads, and learners in Halifax where possible; friendly instructor-learner interactions.
- Use simple, rounded icons for services (e.g. learner lessons, refresher lessons, motorway lessons) to keep the interface intuitive.

### Layout & Feel
- Clear, structured layout with generous white space; easy to skim on mobile.
- Prominent display of reviews and ratings to reinforce "highly rated instructor".
- Subtle motion: soft fade-in or slide-up on scroll for sections (services, reviews, contact), not overwhelming.
- Backgrounds: light gradients or soft geometric patterns that evoke roads/lanes (e.g. diagonal lines, subtle curves) to create depth without clutter.

### Brand Keywords
**Trustworthy, calm, supportive, local to Halifax, highly rated, clear, learner-friendly.**
